Movie Ticket Booking System: Readme
Welcome to the Movie Ticket Booking System! This is a project for booking movie tickets online, providing a convenient and user-friendly experience for both film enthusiasts and theatre owners.

Group members: Rasim Arapov, Nur Islam Kubanychbekov

Rasim made the Dashboard part, database, and CSS

Nurislam made the presentation, sign up/sign in parts

Features:

Browse movies: Easily search for movies by title, genre, release date, or theatre location.
See showtimes: View available showtimes for each movie, including date, time, and theatre.
Select seats: Choose your preferred seats from a visual seating chart, with real-time availability updates.
Payment options: Securely pay for your tickets using various payment gateways.
Account management: Create an account to track your booking history and manage personal details.
Theatre management (optional): For theatre owners, manage movie listings, showtimes, seating layouts, and pricing. (This feature depends on your project implementation)
